About the conference

The Diaspora Learning Network invites you to a two-day international conference

on the role of diaspora communities in global peace, development and humanitarian relief.

Australian and international speakers will explore the unique and growing contribution of diasporas,

who are increasingly recognised as innovators in this space. They will address the following themes:

Human and economic development; Conflict resolution and peace building; Humanitarian

issues and response; Human rights; and Settlement in host countries.

A call for Expressions of Interest to present papers will be released shortly.
