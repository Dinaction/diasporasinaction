About the conference

Diaspora Action Australia and the University of Melbourne's Refugee

Studies Program invite you to a two-day international forum on the role of

diaspora communities in global peace, development and humanitarian relief.

Australian and international speakers will explore the unique

and growing contribution of diasporas, which are increasingly recognised

as the innovators of the humanitarian, peace building and aid sector.

A full program of national and international speakers

will be released over the coming months.
